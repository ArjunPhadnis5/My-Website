This video will teach you how to blink an
arduino in different colors.
Now, I will use a CAD software called Tinkercad
so I do not have to buy anything. The way
we are going to make the Arduino blink
in different colors is we will connect the arduino
a breadboard, then we (in c++) will
send power through one of the ports and then go to
a resistor which will connect to the RGB light.
